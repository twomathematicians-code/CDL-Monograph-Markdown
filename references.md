# References

The monograph cites approximately 167 works from the deep learning, mathematics, and machine learning literature. The complete BibTeX bibliography is in [`references.bib`](references.bib).

Below is a selection of the most-cited primary sources, organized by topic.

## Foundational deep learning

- **LeCun, Bengio, Hinton (2015)**. *Deep learning*. Nature. `lecun2015deep`
- **Goodfellow, Bengio, Courville (2016)**. *Deep Learning*. MIT Press. `goodfellow2016deep`
- **Bengio (2009)**. *Learning deep architectures for AI*. Foundations and Trends in ML. `bengio2009learning`
- **Rumelhart, Hinton, Williams (1986)**. *Learning representations by back-propagating errors*. Nature. `rumelhart1986learning`
